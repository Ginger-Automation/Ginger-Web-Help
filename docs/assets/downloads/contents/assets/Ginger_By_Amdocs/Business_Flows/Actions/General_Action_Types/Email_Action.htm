Introduction 

Email Action is used for sending an email. Attachments can also be used while sending the email.

Email can be sent in two ways:-

Outlook

SMTP

 

How to Configure Action

Click on the Business Flows. Click on the ‘+’ icon as shown below

        

 

Click on ‘Actions Library’

         

 

 

Go to the ‘General Actions’ tab under the ‘Action Library’ screen and double click on Email Action’.

             

 

 

Email Action will be added as shown below

         

 

Double click on it and go to the ‘Operation Settings’ tab.

          

 

 

Users can fill in the details like From, To, CC, Subject, Body and Attachment Files Path as per the requirement.

Go to the ‘Delivery Method’ tab on this screen to select ‘E-mail Method’ as ‘Outlook’.
